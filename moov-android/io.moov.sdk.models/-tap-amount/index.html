TapAmount

data class TapAmount(val amount: ULong, val currency: Currency, val tip: ULong, val surcharge: ULong)

The amount to authorize on a tap, along with an optional tip/surcharge breakdown.

amount is the total the card will be authorized for, expressed in the smallest currency unit (e.g. cents for USD — 100uL is $1.00).

tip and surcharge are descriptive: they are already included in amount and are carried alongside it so the breakdown is preserved end-to-end for reporting. The caller is responsible for folding tip and surcharge into amount before constructing this value; the SDK does not add them on top.

When either component is non-zero, tip + surcharge must be less than or equal to amount. Equality is permitted — it describes a transaction that is entirely tip and/or surcharge with no base purchase.

The invariant is enforced at construction; IllegalArgumentException is thrown when it does not hold.
